## ğŸµ How to Convert a Video (Every Time)

### Step 1: Find Your Video on YouTube

1. Open the YouTube app
2. Find the video you want
3. Tap the **Share** button (under the video)
4. Tap **Copy link**
5. The link is now copied!

### Step 2: Convert to MP3

1. **Open the MP3 Converter app** (the icon you added to home screen)
2. **First time only:** Enter your password and tap "Login"
3. **Tap in the text box** (says "Paste YouTube link here...")
4. **Hold and tap "Paste"** to paste the YouTube link
5. **Tap the purple "Convert to MP3" button**
6. **Wait 30-60 seconds** - you'll see:
   - "Fetching audio..."
   - Then "Converting to MP3..."
   - Then "Download complete!"

### Step 3: Find Your Downloaded MP3

**On iPhone:**
- Open the **Files** app
- Go to **Downloads** folder
- Your MP3 is there!
- You can move it to your Music app

**On Android:**
- Open your **Files** or **Downloads** app
- Your MP3 is in the Downloads folder
- You can play it in your music player
